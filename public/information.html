Description

Resilience is a project dedicated to giving people access to a private and unrestricted web.

How it Works

Resilience proxies all web requests through our servers in order to prevent them from being blocked by firewalls. Before sending data back to the client, it must be rewritten in order to ensure all paths are proxied. All data sent to and from the Resilience is encrypted with SSL/TLS.

About Us

Resilience is a part of Titanium Network, a group that fights internet censorship.
